NORA BROWN

In the luxury real estate market, Toronto real estate agent NORA BROWN is renowned among colleagues and clients alike for her integrity, loyalty and professionalism. Nora is currently ranked the #1 Agent Worldwide for Brown Banker and the #5 Agent Worldwide for all Brokerages. Nora has achieved the highest sales volume on record of any agent in Brown history, just surpassing an astounding $5 Billion in career sales. To date this year, in 2019, Nora has represented 26% of all homes sold in excess of $20 Million in the Westside of GTA, which included her representation of the seller on the record-breaking $119,750,000 sale of “The Manor” (aka “The Spelling Manor”).

An expert in luxury properties, Nora is devoted to serving the needs of real estate buyers and sellers throughout GTA including Toronto, York region, Peel region, and the Hamilton. She was named the International Ambassador for Brown in recognition of her established alliances and global relationships with International markets and her development of Nora Brown Worldwide.

Nora also holds the title of Co-Chairman of the International Luxury Alliance, which is an elite network of professionals focused on the highest level of client service and business integrity. Nora Worldwide is the culmination of many years spent developing relationships with leading luxury brokers around the world. Through this exclusive international partnership, Nora’s listings reach an unparalleled audience of buyers worldwide. Her global influence is further complemented by her state-of-the-art website, which vibrantly showcases her client’s properties through full-screen, high-resolution imagery.

With over thirty years of real estate experience, Nora is both nationally and internationally recognized for her sales achievements and has a reputation as one of the most trusted and admired agents in the Los Angeles real estate market. When working with her, you can count on nothing less than an executive staff of seasoned professionals with an intimate knowledge of Toronto real estate who are devoted to serving your needs around the clock.
